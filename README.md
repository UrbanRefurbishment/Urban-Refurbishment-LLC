Based in Minneapolis, Minnesota, Urban Remodeling provides premium residential renovation services with a strong focus on preserving the charm of older homes. Backed by more than a decade in business and a team with 40 years of collective expertise, the company handles everything from structural upgrades to personalized design solutions. Their process emphasizes quality workmanship, client-focused service, and organized project management through BuilderTrend. Urban Remodeling is a member of NARI and maintains an A+ rating with the BBB.
